What changes in 0.2.4

  • MGES v1

    Separate local in-process performance regression and deterministic governance-boundary conformance profiles, with raw observations, source fingerprints, uncertainty, and publication rules.

  • Provider-neutral tool adapters

    Candidate defineToolAdapter(), registerToolAdapter(), and runToolAdapterConformance() APIs for host-supplied function, SDK, HTTP, MCP-shaped, and custom seams.

  • Stricter adapter declarations

    Handler governance cannot be downgraded, and ambiguous leading or trailing whitespace in governance labels is rejected.

  • ProductLoop composition

    A concrete candidate example registers one static operation through createProductLoopOS().maqamGateway.

  • Launch evidence

    Comparison, roadmap, technical articles, benchmark documentation, and three captioned Remotion videos.
